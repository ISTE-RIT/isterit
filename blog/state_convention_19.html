18th Annual ISTE State Convention

Posted: January 21, 2020

RIT students have secured 2nd prize for the event "Glow it Or Blow it" as a part of 18th Annual ISTE State Convention, held at GEC Barton Hill ,on January 18th and 19th ,2020.

Fourth Year students, Rinu Raju and Shinas Rasheed , branch of Electrical and Electronics Engineering has secured the prize for the event which was a electric circuit wiring competition.

ISTE RIT SB feel proud and we congratulate them for their acheivement..
