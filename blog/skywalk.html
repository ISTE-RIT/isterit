Patching The Skywalk

Posted: May 19, 2021

Kottayam the “Akshara Nagari” has been a city that has been under constant changes and adaptations. One such busy stretches where we can see buzzing activity is the Junction at Sastri Road.

A proposal of a Skywalk to ease the pedestrian rush was initiated by The Roads and Bridges Development Corporation of Kerala but was later on halted for technical reasons.

Patching the Skywalk - re Design the Kottayam Footbridge (ആകാശപാത). You have to design a FOOTBRIDGE at the junction at Sastri Road aiming to create a self-sustaining attraction which enhances the cultural experience of Kottayam.

The design approach must be economically efficient to fabricate, transport, and quick to construct. The design should have simple interfaces to minimize the impact on existing infrastructure and the surrounding context.
