Posted: May 12, 2021

Avengers Infinite Quest

A three round competition was conducted as a part of the Swadhyaya Series to find the greatest marvel fan. The first phase of the quest was an MCQ based quiz that was held with the help of quizizz website at 06:30pm ,134 among the 136 registrations attended the MCQ and 30 among them were selected for the 2nd phase .

The 2nd phase was to write a fanfiction story and 5 among 28 responses were selected for the final phase . The event helped students for stress relief and improved their creative thinking ability .

The 3rd round was made available for non participants to watch as well and was made interactive with the audience as well . 1st prize was awarded to Anandu S Krishnan of NSS College of engineering palakkad. The event was concluded with a thank you message from the secretory of ISTE SC RIT

The Event was coordinated by Sumith Sunil, Nandana Gopan, Lakshmi V, Amal Roy, Mehrunnisa and Resmiya V S.
