ISTE State Convention

Posted: Feb 11, 2018

The always leading richness of ISTE has brought about several pinnacles over the years. And as a further lead to its flourishing, its 16th ISTE State Convention for the year 2018 as SANGRAH ‘18 was held at GCE Kannur. RIT chapter did its best for this convention and was in the top 15 list from around 40+ colleges. We are proud to announce that our students Shinoy Jayan K.D. & Sanjay Krishna J have secured 1st place in technical event named ‘you wire’. It was a wiring competition where circuit lay out and condition to be obtained are given to the participant and the evaluation was strictly based on output, time taken for work, quality of work etc. This highlights the interest of students to participate in more events and also a sign of perfect utilisation of ISTE’s opportunities. We are glad that we could be a platform for them and we congratulate the winners and also those who took part in Sangrah18 from ISTE RIT chapter. We wish we could achieve more.
