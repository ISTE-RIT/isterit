Mechatronics Club

Mechatronics is a multidisplinary field of engineering that includes a combination of Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics and Computer Engineering. The Mechatronics club of Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technolgy is a part of the ISTE RIT chapter and was formed the 1st of november 2015. ISTE Mechatronics club assist the students to develop and apply traditional classroom theory in practical fields. It also promotes opportunities for students to undertake industry experience and assist to strengthen the bond between university, students and industry. Mechatronics club consists of enthusiastic members from mechanical, electrical, electronics and computer engineering departments guided by an advisor and a great team of student board members. The club has conducted numerous workshops which involved active participation from students. This upgrowing club is now planning and taking initiatives in conducting many more workshops, exhibitions and seminars within RIT and among various colleges.
